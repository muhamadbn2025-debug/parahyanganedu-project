Empowering People Through Education

ParahyanganEdu is an online learning platform designed to help students and professionals in Bandung and across Indonesia gain valuable digital skills.

We believe that education should be accessible to everyone. Through our platform, learners can explore various courses in technology, design, and business — without leaving their city.

Thousands of students have already joined ParahyanganEdu to improve their careers and achieve their goals.

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide high quality online education that helps people in Bandung and beyond learn practical skills and grow their careers in the digital world.

We collaborate with experienced instructors and industry experts to deliver courses that are relevant and useful for the local and national job market.

From beginners to professionals, ParahyanganEdu helps learners achieve their learning goals through interactive and engaging content.
